For technical reasons, a test is called an exam on this site.

To be able to attempt an exam, you must first login. If you don't have an account, you must register for an account.

There are 2 kinds of exams:

  • Private Exams

    These exams are uploaded and managed by users of this site. They can control who can give the exam, modify the exam, etc.

  • Public Exams

    These exams are uploaded by users of this site. However they do not manage/control them and you are free to give them.

To attempt an exam, you can click the button. This will start the exam and take you to the cover page of the exam. You can also click on . You can later find the exam in {% if user.is_authenticated %} 'My exams'. {% else %} 'My exams'. {% endif %}

If you close your browser window while an exam is in progress, your progress will not be lost. You can find the exam in 'My exams'.

The exams that you have given in the past can also be found in 'My exams'.
